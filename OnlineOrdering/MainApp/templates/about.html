ABOUT

The main objective of online food ordering system is to automate the existing manual system with the help of advance computerized software so, that valuable data can be stored for longer period with easy accessing and manipulation of the same. Any user can access the Page. User can surf the food items , Cart and Cash on Delivery payment options are available to user. In Online Food Ordering System User can add or remove food items from cart, edit/delete food items, Enable/Disable the food items according to season and availability. Admin have authority to view order details and Fetch database with a unique ID. The payment transaction and user details are also viewable to admin.
